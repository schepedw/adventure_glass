The 2/3 person Pink Flamingo paddleboats are available in the regular version and a self draining version

  • The Adventureglass Paddleboat's are always a success in Parades and such
  • This is a good way to get media attention.
  • Or do it just for fun.

The Perfect tool where your  guests will willingly spend a little more money at your facility without thinking it is too expensive

The Pink flamingo is also available with wings
The Pink Flamingo looks almost alive. The pretty pink color is in the fiberglass for better durability. 
Available Options:
  • Electric motor
  • Stainless Steel Steering and Cranks
  • Canvas cover for the seating area
  • Bimini Top Sunshade
  • Automotive Paint on top of colored fiberglass for extra protection
  • Thicker Hull

Facts about The Pink Flamingo Paddleboat.
  •  Seats 1, 2 or 3 people
  • Length 8 feet
  • Width  5 feet
  • Weight  165 pounds
  • Draft at 600 pounds 11 inches
  • Height without head 36 inches
  • Height with head 79 inches
The Adventureglass Paddleboats are proudly hand made in Indiana USA
The selfbailing version is equipped with The completely SEALED Pontoons which makes the Dragon virtually unsinkable.
Furthermore The pontoons are mounted onto the deck (top) of the boat fastened without interfering with the sealed area of the pontoon, which helps keep the pontoons sealed even over time.
